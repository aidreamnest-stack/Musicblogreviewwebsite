# How to Add New Reviews to TribleTunes

## Quick Start

1. **Click the Floating "+" Button** in the bottom-right corner of any page
2. Fill out the review form with album/song details
3. Click "Publish Review" - your new review will appear on the homepage!

## Adding a Review

### Required Fields:
- **Album/Song Title**: The name of the album or song you're reviewing
- **Artist Name**: The performing artist or band
- **Genre**: Select from available genres (Electronic, Hip Hop, Indie Rock, etc.)
- **Rating**: Rate from 1-10
- **Cover Image URL**: Link to an album cover image (use Unsplash or similar)
- **Short Excerpt**: A brief teaser (shown on cards)
- **Full Review Content**: Your complete review (use double line breaks for paragraphs)

### Optional Fields:
- **YouTube Video ID**: Just the ID part (e.g., "dQw4w9WgXcQ" from youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ)
- **Spotify URL**: Full Spotify link to the album
- **Featured**: Check this to feature the review on the homepage hero section
